Our Achievements

LEED Gold Certification

Our AI Engineering Center in Noida has earned LEED Gold Certification from the U.S. Green Building Council—a global recognition of our commitment to sustainable design and operations.

598 KW Solar Energy System

The Noida campus is powered by a 598 KW solar energy system, supplying nearly 50% of the building’s energy needs. LEED Gold Certification from the U.S. Green Building Council—a global recognition of our commitment to sustainable design and operations.

100% Carbon-Free Electricity

Our headquarters in Redmond, WA operates on 100% carbon-free electricity, further advancing our global sustainability goals.

Cut Water Use by 77% with Smart Facility Design

Zero water discharge facility with advanced rainwater harvesting, achieved a 77% reduction in water usage.

Optimized Building Designs to Save Energy

High-performance façade design and heat-minimizing building materials to reduce energy consumption

Thermal Management Through Reflective Design
