Console Gaming Competition analysis Dashboard

Overview

The console gaming competition analysis dashboard provides the overall performance of the console gaming companies in terms of its various products and curated features and allows deeper comparison of the console gaming based on various attributes derived from their offerings.

Features:

Industry Summary

This page provides the key metrics in terms of volume and revenue for comparison of the overall performance of the console gaming companies.

  • Compare the volume in terms of total games by various console companies
  • Evaluate the volume & revenue trends observed across products by content safety ratings
  • Evaluate the revenue trends & user ratings for the various console products across time

Gaming Ratings

This page provides the key metrics in terms of revenue for comparison of the performance of console products by content safety ratings over the years.

  • Compare & evaluate the revenue trends observed across products by content safety ratings across time

Consoles

This page provides the key metrics in terms of revenue for comparison of the performance of the various popular console products.

  • Compare the revenue earned across the range of popular console products by companies

Genres

This page provides the key metrics in terms of revenue & user scores for comparison of the performance of console products by genre over the years

  • Compare & evaluate the user scores & revenue trends observed across products by genre across time

Survey Results

The page summarizes the various surveys conducted to arrive at the various metrics for evaluation of the console products.
