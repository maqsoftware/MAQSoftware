Retail Chain Global Sales Dashboard

Overview

Showcase the sales performance and profitability of a global retail chain across its various operating geographies. Allows users to switch view between sales and profitability. Breakdown results by country and city. Allow users to view the sales performance across product categories and subcategories and gain perspective of how sales across product divisions are percolating to profits across various geographies.

Features:
  • Allow users to view total sales across product categories and subcategories
  • Displays a summarised view of total sales and profitability across time
  • Allows users to view the total sales and profitability across various geographies
  • Shows the top performing cities from among its operational geographies
  • Ability to switch the view between sales and profitability
  • Compare sales vs profits to evaluate & benchmark company's performance across product divisions and geographies
UI/UX Solution:

In a single dashboard, the Sales dashboard provides the flexibility to the end users to switch perspectives using a single click. By allowing users to switch view providing the top-line and bottom-line growth of the retail chain across various geographies, product divisions & time, they are empowered immensely to take business decisions.
