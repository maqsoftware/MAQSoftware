Session Details

Want to fast-track your Microsoft Fabric migration?

In this session, we will share a proven roadmap that has helped enterprises cut months off their migration timelines. Using a real-world example, we’ll explore how enterprises with large-scale, complex data sources have successfully streamlined Fabric migration.

Gain insights into key phases of migration while learning how to avoid the most common pitfalls. Walk away with a clear, actionable strategy to keep your Fabric project on track, within budget, and set up for long-term success.
