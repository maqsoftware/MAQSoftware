Thermometer

Represent progress toward goals with a highly customizable thermometer graphic.



Visualize your progress towards a target goal with Thermometer by MAQ Software. Using a representational thermometer chart, this visual offers you at-a-glance insights into your KPIs. You can also color-code your progress towards goals with performance zones.


Try it now

Business Uses

  • Manufacturing: Track inventory levels, throughput, rate of return, maintenance costs, uptime, average delivery time, and average number of shipments
  • Retail: Track sales per square foot, return rates, and average spending
  • Human Resources: Track average attendance, employee turnover, and employee satisfaction

Key Features

  • Customizable fill and border color
  • Customizable color-coded performance zones
  • Editable minimum and maximum scale values
  • Ability to turn off the tick bar for a minimalist look
