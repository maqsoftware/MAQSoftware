Key Benefits

Eliminate licensing cost

Power BI per user licenses are not needed, only capacity licenses (P1 - P5). Save costs. No need to assign licenses to users.

Seamless integration

Easily integrate our API into your applications. Embed Power BI without the time or resource complexities.

Highly customizable

Tailor EmbedFAST's capabilities to seamlessly align with your organization's unique needs. Benefit from features that perfectly fit your workflows.

Role-based access management

Effortlessly manage user permissions and data security with built-in role-based access controls.

Scalable & secure

Onboard unlimited customer tenants/workspaces, ensuring data privacy and seamless scalability for your organization.

Free updates and upgrades

Stay up to date with the latest improvements without the need for manual updates.
