About our session

Session: "Load Testing and Embedding in Power BI a Guide for ISVs and Enterprises"

Join our session to accelerate Power BI integration with EmbedFAST and to streamline load testing with LoadFAST.

Learn how to seamlessly embed interactive Power BI reports in your applications, improving user experience, while efficiently managing load tests and capacity planning, regardless of user volume.

Tailored for ISVs and enterprises, this session offers concise insights into improving business efficiency through live demos of our solutions.

About MAQ Software

As a Microsoft Fabric Featured Partner and the winner of the Microsoft Power BI Partner of the Year award, MAQ Software is at the forefront of AI-first development, enabling leading companies to accelerate their business intelligence and analytics initiatives.

Our clients consistently recognize us for our expertise in providing architecture and governance frameworks, implementing best practices to optimize reports, and building team capability through training programs.
